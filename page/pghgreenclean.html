Our Goal is To Provide
Eco-friendly Clean Service

Holly Louise began Pittsburgh Green Clean after seeing the majority of the national cleaning companies advertise green products and services, but were not actually using these cleaning methods at all. At Pittsburgh Green Clean a fresh batch of cleaning products are made each and every visit to your home. These are all crafted with home-based products, likely found in your very own kitchen. Ingredients include: baking soda, white vinegar, washing soda, natural cleansers, and lots of elbow grease. Essential oils such as On Guard and Wild Orange are added as a natural household surface cleaners
